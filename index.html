Argentina

Argentina, located in South America, is a vibrant and diverse nation known for its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and passionate people. Spanning over 2.7 million square kilometers, it’s the eighth-largest country in the world, bordered by Chile, Bolivia, Paraguay, Brazil, Uruguay, and the Atlantic Ocean. Its capital, Buenos Aires, is a bustling metropolis famous for tango, European-style architecture, and vibrant nightlife. Argentina’s geography ranges from the Andes mountains in the west to the fertile Pampas plains and Patagonia’s rugged beauty in the south.
